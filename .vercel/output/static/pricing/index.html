Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my listing?

Find your business on one of our county directory sites, click "Claim This Listing," and verify your ownership. Once approved, you can update your info, add photos, and post deals — all for free.

Can I post deals with the free Verified listing?

Yes! Every verified listing can post coupons and deals to attract customers. Upgrade to Featured for even more visibility and features.

What's included in the annual plan?

All paid plans offer an annual option with 2 months free. That's 12 months of service for the price of 10. Cancel anytime.

Does my listing appear on all county sites?

Your listing appears on the county directory where your business is located. Multi-location businesses can have separate listings on each county site.

What does the SEO Package include?

Our Full SEO Package includes professional website redesign, Google Business Profile optimization, quarterly highlight articles, and ongoing local SEO strategy — powered by MapPulse Agency.

Can I upgrade or cancel anytime?

Start free and upgrade whenever you're ready. Monthly plans can be canceled at any time — your listing reverts to the free tier at the end of your billing period.
